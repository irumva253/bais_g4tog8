About
Bright Angels International School

Bright Angels International School seeks to enlighten children through the power of Christian Education .We strive to make sure our students are held to the highest level of discipline while providing a quality education that encourages and complies with Godly principles.

Vision: To raise children up with good intellectual abilities and character, firmly grounded in the faith of Jesus Christ.

Our Goal: To give each student the tools they need to achieve their highest potential and lead their lives in a way that brings honor to God.

Phyilosopy: Bright Angels International School believes that good quality education combined with good character, discipline and living a life of faith that honors God's will increases the chance of success in life. Education given by Bright Angels International School provides children with the materials to understand the world and learn from it. Good character ,discipline and living a life of faith will provide the tools and guidance to apply those materials to make wise and informed decisions in life, as well as conquer life's challenges.

Our Children will have the knowledge and capacity to interpret their surroundings and use their gifts to make a positive impact in the world. These attributes, accompanied with faith, will provide the moral courage and spiritual strength to accept greater responsibilities and guide individuals towards a successful life.
